The Fajardo Method™

THE FAJARDO METHOD OF HOLISTIC BIOMECHANICS™

The Fajardo Method of Holistic Biomechanics™ was created by Alicia Fajardo, owner of Transformations Studio in Portland, Oregon. This modality focuses on nervous system health and biomechanics, which include body structure, alignment, and healthy, supported movement. Because biomechanics are dictated by the nervous system state, understanding basic nervous system function and how the nervous system responds to stress is necessary for understanding the modality.
