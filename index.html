The Fajardo Method of Holistic Biomechanics™ teaches movement education and motor re-patterning in conjunction with nervous system awareness.
This Fajardo Method™ is appropriate for a wide variety of issues. Some clients of the modality are addressing stress and anxiety. Some wish to avoid surgery, while others are experiencing discomfort in their body or lack of energy. This Fajardo Method™ is also effective for highly accomplished athletes and individuals who want to improve their movement biomechanics for skill efficiency or overall health and wellness.

OVERALL HEALTH & WELLNESS

If you are injured, experiencing chronic pain, considering surgical intervention, or if your body is simply not functioning well, there is a good chance that you have some non-optimal movement patterns or poor biomechanics contributing to the issue.

This modality is appropriate for athletes or anyone who wants to improve their movement biomechanics for skill efficiency, injury prevention, or overall health and wellness.

The nervous system state (sympathetic or parasympathetic) dictates digestive function, immune system function, cardiovascular health, and more. Learn about the mechanisms that affect these aspects of health.

A LEARNING PROCESS

The Fajardo Method™ is an educational process as well as movement training. Clients learn about the anatomy and physiology of the body, about how the nervous system functions and creates movement, and how to identify whether or not they are moving in biomechanically-optimally ways and how to create new motor patterns.
